About Me

I'm Nathan Saunders — a software developer working at Soft-Where and Vorwo. My main focus right now is AI automation for businesses.

I work as a developer at Soft-Where, where I help build and maintain ERP software that businesses use to manage their operations. My day-to-day involves backend development, building integrations, and automating workflows inside the platform.

Through Vorwo, I focus on AI automation for businesses. The idea is simple — most businesses have processes that take up a lot of manual time and could be handled by software if it were set up right. That's what I work on. Figuring out where automation makes sense and then actually building it.

Outside of that I'm currently building a stock market app using the Alpaca API and a handful of open-source libraries from GitHub. It's a personal project that combines my interest in markets with the kind of software work I enjoy doing.

AI automation is my main area of interest and where I spend most of my time. I'm always looking to take on new projects — if you want to work on something, reach out at nathan@vorwo.com.
